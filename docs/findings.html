1.1 1.1 AI‚Äôs Impact on Different Industries

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping industries, leading to both job displacement and job creation. According to the World Economic Forum (2024), AI will impact 85 million jobs but simultaneously create 97 million new ones (Forum (2024)).

1.2 üìä Interactive Bar Chart: AI Job Impact by Industry

Code 
import pandas as pd
import plotly.express as px

# Industry-wise job impact data
data = {
    "Industry": ["Tech & Data Science", "Finance", "Manufacturing", "Retail & Customer Service", "Healthcare", "Marketing & Advertising"],
    "Jobs Created": [500000, 300000, 250000, 200000, 400000, 150000],
    "Jobs Lost": [50000, 150000, 300000, 500000, 50000, 80000]
}

df = pd.DataFrame(data)

# Create interactive bar chart
fig = px.bar(
    df,
    x="Industry",
    y=["Jobs Created", "Jobs Lost"],
    barmode="group",
    title="<b>AI‚Äôs Impact on Job Growth vs. Loss by Industry</b>",
    labels={
        "Industry": "<i>Industry</i>",
        "value": "<i>Number of Jobs</i>",
        "variable": "<i>Job Impact</i>"
    },
    color_discrete_sequence=px.colors.qualitative.Bold
)

fig.update_layout(
    font=dict(family="Arial", size=14),
    title_font_size=20,
    plot_bgcolor="rgba(240,240,240,0.9)",
    paper_bgcolor="rgba(240,240,240,0.95)",
    legend_title_text="<b>Job Category</b>",
    xaxis=dict(showgrid=False),
    yaxis=dict(showgrid=True, gridcolor="lightgrey", zeroline=False)
)

fig.update_traces(marker_line_width=1.5, marker_line_color='black')

fig.show()

‚úÖ Finding: AI is not just eliminating jobs‚Äîit is reshaping job roles and requiring new skill sets (Online (2024)).
